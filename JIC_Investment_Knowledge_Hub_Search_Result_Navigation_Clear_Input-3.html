Internal Purpose

Main function of this internal application

This application is a lightweight internal reference portal: long PDFs, strategic books, and investment guides are transformed into structured pages that can be searched, bookmarked, printed, and read comfortably by JIC staff, DPMPTSP, and government teams on mobile, tablet, laptop, or large displays.

For JIC internal staffFind strategic context, licensing concepts, KBLI references, investment opportunities, and source documents for briefings and service support.
For JIC teamsUse one adaptive internal reading hub instead of scattered PDFs and slide decks.
For DPMPTSP & government teamsCompare Jakarta RISE targets, sustainability initiatives, sector guides, and implementation priorities in one search interface.
